Accessibility Statement

PasswordsForge.com is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. If you encounter any accessibility issues or need assistance using our site, please contact us at passwordsforge@gmail.com.

We aim to continually improve the user experience and apply the relevant accessibility standards where possible.

Our site supports keyboard navigation and screen readers, and we strive to use clear colors and large fonts. If you need any specific adjustment, please let us know.
